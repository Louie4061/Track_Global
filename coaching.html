Coaching with Macatack

MacAtack is an online athletics coaching service designed specifically for sprinters and mid-distance runners. Offering personalized training plans, expert guidance, and performance tracking, MacAtack helps athletes optimize their training and reach their full potential. With a focus on sprinting technique, speed development, endurance, and race strategy, the service tailors programs to suit individual needs, whether for competitive athletes or those looking to improve fitness and technique. MacAtack provides access to experienced coaches, video analysis, and a supportive online community to foster growth and success in track and field events.

About coach Jake

As a junior athlete, Jake was a national medalist and, at the age of 17, ran his personal best in the 400m with a time of 49.17 seconds. While injuries have kept him away from the track for a time, he’s now on a journey to return to competitive sprinting for the upcoming 2025 season!

Throughout his career as an elite athlete, Jake has gained invaluable knowledge in speed development, sprinting mechanics, and tailoring training to suit the individual athlete. His coach of over 10 years has taught him the essential qualities of becoming an elite sprinter and provided a blueprint for how a successful coach should operate.

Though training sprinters is Jake’s primary focus, his role as a Playbook Coach has led him to work with athletes from other sports, including football and soccer, who want to improve their speed and agility. This experience has taught him how to adapt his coaching methods to meet the needs of athletes from different disciplines, all of whom have responded very positively to the training provided.

Jake primarily coaches around Western-Sydney NSW Australia but may be avaliable for online coaching

Jake is a certified Athletics Australia Level 1 Coach. Combined with his experience as a successful athlete, he believes these qualifications will allow him to offer invaluable support to athletes looking to enhance their speed and performance.
